# Qatra — Privacy Policy (سياسة الخصوصية)

## Last Updated: 2026-07-13

---

## 1. من نحن (Who We Are)
Qatra (قطرة) is a community blood donor directory platform for Algeria. We connect blood donors with patients in need.

## 2. البيانات التي نجمعها (Data We Collect)

### 2.1 بيانات الم Donateur (Donor Data)
- **الاسم الكامل** (Full Name) — displayed publicly
- **رقم الهاتف** (Phone Number) — used for Call/WhatsApp links, stored encrypted
- **نوع الدم** (Blood Type) — displayed publicly
- **الولاية** (Wilaya) — displayed publicly
- **البلدية** (Commune) — displayed publicly
- **رابط فيسبوك** (Facebook URL) — optional, displayed publicly
- **تاريخ آخر تبرع** (Last Donation Date) — displayed publicly

### 2.2 بيانات أخرى (Other Data)
- **التقييمات** (Ratings) — stars + optional comment
- **الملاحظات** (Feedback) — optional suggestions
- **طلبات الدم** (Blood Requests) — patient info for urgent needs

## 3. كيف نستخدم بياناتك (How We Use Your Data)
- Connect donors with patients in need
- Display donor information in the public directory
- Process blood donation requests
- Improve the platform based on feedback

## 4. مشاركة البيانات (Data Sharing)
- **Public Directory:** Donor information is publicly visible by design
- **No Third Parties:** We do not sell or share data with third parties
- **No Analytics:** We do not use tracking or analytics tools

## 5. أمن البيانات (Data Security)
- Database encrypted at rest (Supabase)
- HTTPS for all connections
- Row-Level Security (RLS) enforced
- Input validation and sanitization
- Content Security Policy (CSP) implemented
- No server-side logs of user activity

## 6. حقوقك (Your Rights)
You have the right to:
- **Access:** View all your stored data
- **Correction:** Update your information
- **Deletion:** Delete your account and all associated data
- **Objection:** Object to data processing

## 7. حذف الحساب (Account Deletion)
To delete your account:
1. Open the Qatra application
2. Scroll to the footer
3. Click "حذف حسابي" (Delete My Account)
4. Enter your phone number
5. Confirm deletion

Your data will be permanently deleted from our database within 24 hours.

## 8. ملفات تعريف الارتباط (Cookies)
Qatra does not use cookies. We use browser localStorage only for:
- Language preference (ar/en)
- UI state (modals, pagination)

## 9. الأطفال (Children)
Qatra is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
